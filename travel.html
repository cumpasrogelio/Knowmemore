places i've been

On the pictures you can easily notice the places I've been. Since childhood I've been exploring the beautiful spots here in Baguio and Benguet. I've been at Our Lady of Atonement Cathedral, Burnham Park, Philippine Military Academy, Pink Sisters’ Convent and Chapel, Our Lady of Lourdes Grotto, Tam-Awan Village, Mines View Park, Botanical Garden, Bell Church, Camp John Hay, Asin Hot Springs, Diplomat Hotel, The Mansion, Lion's Head, Laperal White House and many other more. During summer we also visit our relatives at La Union. When my grandparents were still around, once a year we visit our relatives at Batangas City and Tacloban City. And sometimes if my wife and I have a money, we eat on different kinds of restaurants.
