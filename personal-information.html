Welcome to my life ! :)

First of all let me introduce my self, I'm Rogelio de Vera Cumpas Jr from La Trinidad, Benguet. I have been staying here for almost all of my life. I was born January 28, 1994 and as of now I'm 21 years old.  Five (5) feet and four (4) inches in height, weight's 57 kilograms. My color is brown and the hair is black. My Grandmother is from Tacloban city, Grandfather is from Batangas City, my Father was born at Manila and my Mother is from here at Benguet. Basically i don't know what race I will be included, i'll let you guys decide for that :). 

summary of my life

Back when i was a toddler, I used to stay with my Grandparents and  accompany  them on their house. As time goes by, I graduated from elementary and returned to my parents house to study in high school . I graduated in high school and studied at TESDA  eventually I got a job. For four years I worked hard, but the salary was not enough to fulfill the needs of my family and the needs of my daughter. I wanted to study in college but i didn't have that much money until an opportunity was given to me by the parents of my wife  who is currently paying my bills at the university. Now I'm doing my best to finish my studies so that I can repay the people who helped me and also to give my family a better life.

Interest

Some of my hobbies are jogging, playing  basketball and playing  soccer because doing these regular activities makes myself healthy. And keeping your self healthy is like keeping your promises in the future (because how can you make you're promise when your dead haha ).
I'm also a fan of playing  computer games, I find playing computer games very interesting and for me it is like exercising my brain (it's just a hunch though hehe). I also have the passion of repairing computers and laptop, sometimes I do it for free but most of the time not.
Every sunday my wife, daughter and I goes to church to pray and give thanks for good health and blessing that  the lord is giving to us. After the mass it's time for family bonding.

Life as a Father

At first I was not quite ready to become a father but with the help of my wife and parents I slowly recognize the worth of having a daughter. When you feel down  and lonely, and see your daughter smile or laugh, the pain goes away. Life becomes more happier and more meaningful when you have someone to cherish.
As time goes by, my daughter became my motivation and my strength. 
I'm not saying that my website visitors should go and make a baby . I'm just sharing what it feels like to be a father, and what it feels like to be me. That's all for my personal information cheers ! :)
